Journey Between Two Worlds!

Trapped in a world with no through road, you have to safely navigate between two parallel universes in order to escape. Are you ready to slip into another dimension, walk through walls, fly through air, and trudge through lava and vines to break free?




Beware of Lava & Traps!

Be careful when you traverse between these two worlds as there is danger everywhere. You will be dealt damage if you land on prickly vines or scorching lava, or lose a life if you fall into a bottomless pit. Getting stuck in walls is also a pain, so make sure to switch worlds at the right place!




Stuck on a Level?

The app offers 50 mind-bending puzzles plus 50 bonus levels. It's time to put on your thinking cap!





About the Developer

The Inverted game is the brainchild of Lucas Tan, a 7th grader in Palo Alto, California. He enjoys making online games and animated stories, and sharing them with his friends. He started developing games on Scratch and more recently in JavaScript and Swift. Inverted is a combnation of two games, "Pixel" and "Parallel Worlds", that he made on Scratch.
