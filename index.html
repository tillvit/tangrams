Journey Between Two Worlds!

Guide the yellow cube through intricate mazes and dangerous paths to its destination. Oftentimes, there is no obvious path forward and you need to travel between two parallel universes to get closer to where you need to go. Good luck!




Beware of Lava & Traps!

Be careful when you traverse between these two worlds as danger abounds. You will be dealt damage by prickly vines, scorching lava and bottomless pits. Getting stuck in walls is also a pain, so make sure to switch worlds at the right place!
